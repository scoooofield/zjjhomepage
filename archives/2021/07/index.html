About me

I am a first-year PhD student at the College of Information and Engineering, Zhejiang University of Technology. I received the BS degree in automation from the Zhejiang University of Technology, Hangzhou, China, in 2018.

Research Interests

My research interests lie primarily in the area of Network Science, Graph Data Mining and Machine Learning, especially for network security.

Machine learning for graph-structured data

  1. Designing effective data augmentation strategies for graphs.
  2. Leveraging data augmentation to learn robust graph representations.
  3. Developing graph learning methods than can efficiently and effectively analyze blockchain data.
